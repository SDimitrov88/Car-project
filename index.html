01 Lamborghini Huracán

The Lamborghini Huracán is a true icon among supercars – combining aggressive design, a naturally aspirated V10 engine and an unadulterated sense of speed. With its 5.2-liter V10 engine developing up to 640 horsepower, this model offers explosive acceleration and a sound that remains unforgettable. Acceleration from 0 to 100 km/h takes about 2.9 seconds, and the top speed exceeds 325 km/h, which places it among the fastest cars in the world.

02 Ford Mustang GT

The Ford Mustang GT is a symbol of American muscle power and pure driving pleasure. With its 5.0-liter V8 engine, developing over 450 horsepower, this car offers impressive acceleration and a characteristic deep sound that cannot be mistaken.Acceleration from 0 to 100 km/h takes about 4 seconds, and the rear-wheel drive provides a classic sporty feel and excellent road control. The design of the Mustang GT is aggressive and recognizable - a long hood, a low profile and the iconic horse grille, which emphasizes its heritage.

Price $60000.00

The new BMW 7 Series (G70) is BMW’s flagship in the luxury sedan class and represents the latest generation of technology, comfort and power. The model has been in production since 2022 and is available with both gasoline and hybrid engines, as well as in the all-electric i7 version. Under the hood, various options are available – from 6-cylinder engines to powerful V8s and hybrid systems with an output of up to around 571 horsepower.
